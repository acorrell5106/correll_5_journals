What is Orange?

This is my orange webpage. As you can see, orange is a friendly and fun color. This is because orange represents a cheerful and stimulating environment. Orange is used to convey a sense of confidence when used in marketing, such as Amazon and Firefox. In a webpage, orange might be used for an energetic and exciting website.
