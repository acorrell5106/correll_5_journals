What is Yellow?

This is my yellow webpage. As you can see, yellow is an optimistic and intense color. This is because yellow represents a warm and confident environment. Yellow is used to convey a sense of happiness when used in marketing, such as McDonalds, or it can be used to catch you attention, such as C.A.T. In a webpage, yellow might be used for a happy and inviting webpage, or an attention-grabbing website.
