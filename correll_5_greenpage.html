What is Green?

This is my green webpage. As you can see, green is a natural and peaceful color. This is because green represents a healthy and refreshing environment. Green is used to convey a sense of tranquility when used in marketing, such as Animal Planet and Girl Scouts. In a webpage, green might be used for a natural and relaxing website.
