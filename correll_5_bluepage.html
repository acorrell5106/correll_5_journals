What is Blue?

This is my blue webpage. As you can see, blue is a calm and focused color. This is because blue represents a serene and efficient environment. Blue is used to convey a sense of cool and intelligence when used in marketing, such as AT&T and Facebook. In a webpage, blue might be used for a calm working environment.
